    How the reliefs work (plain English)

    EIS (Enterprise Investment Scheme)

    • 30% income tax relief on qualifying investments up to GBP 1,000,000 per tax year.
    • You must hold the shares for at least 3 years or relief can be clawed back.
    • Relief can be carried back to the previous tax year if you have unused income tax.
    • CGT deferral relief may be available, but is not calculated here.

    SEIS (Seed Enterprise Investment Scheme)

    • 50% income tax relief on qualifying investments up to GBP 200,000 per tax year.
    • Shares must be held for at least 3 years or relief can be clawed back.
    • Relief can be carried back to the previous tax year if you have unused income tax.
    • Additional CGT reliefs may be available; not calculated here.

    VCT (Venture Capital Trust)

    • 20% income tax relief on qualifying investments up to GBP 200,000 per tax year.
    • Relief is only for the current tax year and cannot be carried back.
    • Shares must be held for at least 5 years or relief can be clawed back.
    • Dividends from VCTs are tax-free, but this is not modelled here.
